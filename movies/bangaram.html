Gold. Action. Romance. A cult classic born.

Director: Shiva Nageswara Rao Music: Mani Sharma Genre: Action Thriller Language: Telugu

About the Film

Bangaram (2006) is a Telugu action thriller directed by Shiva Nageswara Rao, starring Pawan Kalyan and Meera Jasmine. The film is a remake of the Tamil film Ghilli and became one of the biggest hits of Pawan Kalyan's career.

The film broke multiple box office records and had a phenomenal theatrical run. It is widely regarded as one of Pawan Kalyan's most iconic films and has a massive cult following even today.

Pawan Kalyan's performance — especially his action sequences and romantic scenes — drew enormous praise. The energy he brought to the screen in Bangaram is considered one of his career-best showings.

Story

Bangaram follows Arjun, a carefree young man who ends up protecting a girl, Meenakshi (Meera Jasmine), from a powerful gangster who wants her for himself. What begins as an accidental situation turns into a thrilling chase, with Arjun fighting against all odds to protect the woman he falls in love with. The film blends high-energy action with romance and comedy in classic Pawan Kalyan style.
