About the Film

Ustaad Bhagat Singh (2026) is the blockbuster reunion of Pawan Kalyan with director Harish Shankar — the same duo behind the mega-hit Gabbar Singh (2012). The wait was worth every second.

Devi Sri Prasad returned on music, completing the golden trio. The album was an explosive chartbuster, with every track becoming a mass anthem the moment it dropped. DSP's energy combined with PK's aura made the music an event in itself.

Released in 2026, Ustaad Bhagat Singh delivered everything fans wanted — Harish Shankar's punchy dialogues, whistle-worthy action, and Pawan Kalyan at his most charismatic and electrifying.

Story

Ustaad Bhagat Singh is a mass entertainer in the truest sense — celebrating Pawan Kalyan's iconic appeal while delivering a story with heart and purpose.
