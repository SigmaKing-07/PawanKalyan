About the Film

Thammudu (1999) is a Telugu action drama directed by S.V. Krishna Reddy, starring Pawan Kalyan in a powerful lead role. The film became one of the biggest hits of 1999 and is remembered as one of Pawan Kalyan's most energetic and entertaining mass performances of his early career.

The film showcased Pawan Kalyan's trademark mix of action, comedy, and emotion. His screen presence in Thammudu was electric, and fans went crazy for his style, dialogue delivery, and fight sequences. The film established the "mass hero" image that would define his career.

Story

The film tells the story of a young man who stands up against injustice to protect his family and loved ones. It is a classic Telugu mass entertainer — packed with action, humor, romance, and emotion — with Pawan Kalyan at the center of every scene, commanding the screen with complete authority.
