Director: Saagar K. Chandra Music: Thaman S Genre: Action Drama Language: Telugu

About the Film

Bheemla Nayak (2022) is a Telugu action drama directed by Saagar K. Chandra, starring Pawan Kalyan and Rana Daggubati in lead roles. The film is a remake of the Malayalam blockbuster Ayyappanum Koshiyum (2020).

The film was one of the most anticipated Telugu releases of 2022. Two powerhouse stars sharing the screen created enormous buzz, and the film delivered spectacularly. The rivalry between Pawan Kalyan's Bheemla and Rana's Daniel Shekar became the highlight of the film.

Thaman S's pulsating background score and the two leads' intense performances made Bheemla Nayak a cinematic event. It became one of the biggest hits of 2022 and further cemented Pawan Kalyan's mass-action star status.

Story

Bheemla Nayak (Pawan Kalyan) is a police constable — a proud, honest man of the people. Daniel Shekar (Rana Daggubati) is a wealthy, powerful ex-army officer with an ego to match. A chance encounter between these two alpha personalities sparks a bitter conflict that escalates into a full-blown war. The film is a gripping character study of masculinity, ego, and consequences.
