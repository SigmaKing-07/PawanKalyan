Bro

A second chance at life. A new perspective on everything.

Director: Samuthirakani Music: Thaman S Genre: Fantasy Comedy Drama Language: Telugu

About the Film

Bro (2023) is a Telugu fantasy comedy drama directed by Samuthirakani, starring Pawan Kalyan and Sai Dharam Tej. The film is the Telugu remake of the Tamil hit Vikram Vedha — wait, actually it's the remake of Oh My Kadavule (2020).

The film features Pawan Kalyan in a unique role as Lord Yama (the God of Death), a departure from his usual action-hero roles. This multi-dimensional character allowed PK to showcase a completely different side of his acting — playful, philosophical, and powerful all at once.

Co-starring his nephew Sai Dharam Tej, the film had a special emotional connection for fans given the real-life family bond between the two stars. Thaman S composed the music, adding energy and depth to the fantasy elements.

Story

A young man (Sai Dharam Tej) is given a rare second chance at life by Yama (Pawan Kalyan) to correct his mistakes. Armed with the ability to go back in time, he tries to set things right in his life — only to discover that fixing one thing breaks another. The film is a heartwarming, funny, and thought-provoking journey about choices, relationships, and what truly matters in life.

Why It's Special

  • Pawan Kalyan plays Lord Yama — a rare mythological character role for him
  • Real-life uncle-nephew duo of PK and Sai Dharam Tej on screen together
  • Unique fantasy premise blending humor and philosophy
  • Thaman S's energetic and melodious soundtrack
  • A refreshing change of pace from typical mass-action entertainers
