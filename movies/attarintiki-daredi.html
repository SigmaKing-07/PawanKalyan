The biggest Telugu blockbuster of its era

Director: Trivikram Srinivas Music: Devi Sri Prasad Genre: Family Drama Language: Telugu

About the Film

Attarintiki Daredi (2013) is a Telugu family drama directed by Trivikram Srinivas, starring Pawan Kalyan and Samantha Ruth Prabhu. It became the highest-grossing Telugu film of its time, breaking every record in the book.

The film was a watershed moment for Telugu cinema — it proved that a family-oriented entertainer without excessive action could be a massive box office phenomenon. Pawan Kalyan's warm, charming performance as Sathyam won the hearts of every generation.

The film ran for 150+ days across multiplexes and single screens alike, with sell-out shows for weeks. It grossed over ₹100 crore worldwide — a historic feat for Telugu cinema at the time.

Story

Sathyam (Pawan Kalyan) is a charming young man sent on a mission by his grandfather to reunite with their estranged family. In the process, he falls in love with Sashi (Samantha), the granddaughter of the family patriarch. The story beautifully navigates family bonds, love, and reconciliation — all wrapped in Trivikram's trademark wit and warmth.
