About the Film

OG (2025) is a pan-India action thriller directed by Sujeeth — known for Saaho — starring Pawan Kalyan in one of his most intense and raw avatars. The film was made on a massive scale with production values that pushed the boundaries of Telugu cinema.

The title OG perfectly captures the aura of Pawan Kalyan — the original, the icon, the one who set the template. Thaman S delivered a power-packed score that amplified the film's high-octane energy from the very first frame.

Released in 2025, the film delivered a massive theatrical experience and lived up to the colossal expectations fans had built around it. PK's screen presence was at its absolute peak, reminding audiences why he is truly the Power Star.

Story

OG follows a larger-than-life protagonist who takes on a dangerous and powerful enemy in a high-stakes action thriller. The film blends gripping action, drama, and Pawan Kalyan's trademark mass appeal into a cinematic spectacle that kept audiences on the edge of their seats from start to finish.
