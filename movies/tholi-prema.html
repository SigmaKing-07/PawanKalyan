Tholi Prema

First love. Forever remembered.

Director: A. Karunakaran Music: Devi Sri Prasad Genre: Romantic Drama Language: Telugu

About the Film

Tholi Prema (1998) is a Telugu romantic drama directed by A. Karunakaran, starring Pawan Kalyan and Keerthi Reddy. The film marked a major milestone in Pawan Kalyan's early career and remains one of the most cherished romantic films of the late 1990s.

The film featured the debut music of Devi Sri Prasad, who went on to become one of the most celebrated music composers in Indian cinema. His soulful soundtrack for Tholi Prema was an instant hit and is still fondly remembered by fans.

Pawan Kalyan's portrayal of a young man deeply in love won hearts across Telugu states and established him as a romantic hero to watch. The film's emotional depth and natural performances gave it a timeless quality.

Story

The film follows a young man who falls deeply in love with a girl from his college. Their romance is pure and sincere, but faces opposition from family and circumstances. The story captures the innocence of first love, the pain of separation, and the power of genuine emotion — all delivered with Pawan Kalyan's natural and heartfelt performance.

Why It's Special

  • Devi Sri Prasad's debut as music composer — a legendary career-starting moment
  • One of the most beloved early Pawan Kalyan films among fans
  • Established PK as a full-fledged romantic hero in Telugu cinema
  • Soundtrack remains iconic — songs still played at weddings and events today
  • Won the Filmfare Award for Best Telugu Film
