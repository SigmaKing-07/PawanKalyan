About the Film

Vakeel Saab (2021) is a Telugu legal drama directed by Venu Sriram, starring Pawan Kalyan in the role of a lawyer. The film is the Telugu remake of the acclaimed Bollywood film Pink (2016), which starred Amitabh Bachchan.

This was Pawan Kalyan's comeback film after a four-year break from cinema due to his political activities. The anticipation was enormous, and he delivered — with a restrained, powerful performance that earned widespread critical praise as one of the best of his career.

The film dealt with serious social issues around consent and women's rights, making it not just an entertainer but an important social commentary. The courtroom sequences were electrifying, with Pawan Kalyan's dialogues delivering maximum impact.

Story

Three young women are falsely accused after a night out goes wrong. Lawyer Satyadev (Pawan Kalyan) takes up their case and fights against a powerful family in court. The film makes a bold statement: a woman's "No" is always final, regardless of circumstances. The courtroom drama builds to a powerful climax that left audiences cheering.
