Hari Hara Veeramallu

A warrior from the Mughal era. A legend for all time.

Director: Krish Jagarlamudi Music: M.M. Keeravani Genre: Period Action Language: Telugu

About the Film

Hari Hara Veeramallu (2025) is one of the most ambitious period action films ever made in Telugu cinema, starring Pawan Kalyan in a historic warrior role. Directed by Krish Jagarlamudi — known for Kanche and Gautamiputra Satakarni — the film is set in the grand Mughal era.

The legendary M.M. Keeravani — Oscar winner for Naatu Naatu from RRR — composed the music, making it one of the most prestigious collaborations in Telugu cinema history. The result was a majestic, sweeping score that matched the grandeur of the film's visuals.

Released in 2025, Hari Hara Veeramallu delivered a spectacular cinematic experience. Pawan Kalyan in period costume, wielding a sword against the Mughal empire, was everything fans had dreamed of — and more.

Story

Set during the Mughal period in Indian history, the film follows the heroic warrior Hari Hara Veeramallu — a fearless rebel who fights for the people against the might of the Mughal empire. It is a story of valor, justice, and the indomitable spirit of a people's hero — crafted on a grand cinematic canvas.
