Sardaar Gabbar Singh

Written by the star himself — raw, bold, and powerful

Director: Bobby Music: Devi Sri Prasad Genre: Action Comedy Language: Telugu / Hindi

About the Film

Sardaar Gabbar Singh (2016) is a Telugu action comedy directed by Bobby, with the story and screenplay written by Pawan Kalyan himself. The film is a sequel to the 2012 hit Gabbar Singh and stars Pawan Kalyan alongside Kajal Aggarwal.

The film was released simultaneously in Telugu and Hindi, with Pawan Kalyan dubbing for himself in Hindi as well. It was a massive commercial success, especially in the Hindi belt, establishing Pawan Kalyan as a pan-India star.

The fact that Pawan Kalyan wrote the story himself makes this film especially significant — it reflects his creative vision and his desire to tell stories that resonate with the common man.

Story

Gabbar Singh (Pawan Kalyan), a fun-loving police officer, is posted in Rajasthan where he takes on a powerful local villain who is terrorizing the village. Along the way, he falls for a feisty local girl (Kajal Aggarwal). The story blends Rajasthani culture, comedy, romance, and action in a unique package that showcased Pawan Kalyan's creative writing abilities.

  • Bilingual release in Telugu and Hindi simultaneously
  • Devi Sri Prasad's music was a massive hit — Sardaar theme became iconic
